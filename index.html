Operational Metrics Review

Operational evaluation of store performance over 30 days shows total sales volume growth of up to Rp735.9 Million (up 39.77%). While the daily chart trend was stable, the primary focus was on controlling the order cancellation metric, which experienced fluctuating escalation at the end of the period.

Daily Operational Control
  • Cancellation Management: Evaluate and reduce the cancel order ratio to avoid store operational penalties to keep account performance healthy (green).
  • Order Fulfillment Cycle: Monitor daily chart spikes to ensure physical stock readiness aligns with warehouse team packing speed.
  • Order Value (AOV): Optimizing the savings combo feature to support high-value sales figures per order (average Rp. 7.5 million/order).
Operational Impact

Through strict monitoring of daily order status and regular stock monitoring within the Shopee Business Suite, the warehouse team's work efficiency is well-controlled to ensure the satisfaction of premium product buyers.

Focus: Business Suite, Order Fulfillment, Operational Control
Operational Health & Fulfillment Reporting
Fulfillment Performance Standard

Shopee's penalty metrics-based monitoring system is implemented to process the logistics team's daily operational data. The focus is on ensuring that bulk order fulfillment is fast, transparent, and measurable.

SLA & Penalty Tracking Metrics
  • Late Shipment Rate (LSR): Maintain the level of late delivery to a minimum to avoid system penalty points.
  • Non-Fulfillment Rate (NFR): Strict monitoring of automatic cancellation by the system due to operational negligence in packaging goods.
  • Real-time Reporting: Provides periodic data for management to monitor the daily workload of the warehouse team during major campaign periods.
Strategic Outcome

This periodic reporting successfully reduces bulk order processing time (*SLA fulfillment time*), keeps account reputation green in the eyes of Shopee's algorithm, and provides clear performance visibility for store owners.

Focus: SLA Control, Store Health, Mass Fulfillment
Ads Performance Monitoring & Budget Control Framework
Budget Efficiency & Controlling

This spreadsheet-based external monitoring system is used to conduct periodic expenditure audits on advertising cost efficiency across methods (Automatic vs Manual), in order to reduce risk. overspending.

Optimization Log Framework
  • Budget Allocation: Controlling the efficiency of monthly tactical advertising cost allocation to be right on target for the highest product conversion.
  • Sales Contribution Evaluation: Map out the real performance comparison between automatic campaigns and group ads to cut down on unproductive ad spend (*leaking budget*).
  • Data Logging: Recap crucial metrics such as CTR (5.14%), Number of Clicks, and Order Volume independently for transparency of management reporting.
Strategic Outcome

This disciplined periodic data recording ensures the stability of advertising capital is neatly controlled, maintains optimal ROI, and provides clear and reliable performance visibility for stakeholders.

Focus: Budget Auditing, CIR Management, Performance Tracking
